Boundary Layer Problem

  1. Write down all the governing equations.
  1. Simplify all the equations.
    1. Intuitive arguments (Guess works and hypothesis)
    1. Scale Analysis (Mostly used for verification purposes)
  1. Write down all the governing Eqns and boundary conditions of problem statement. Also mention the conditions that were assumed in case of ‘sub-cases’ such as high ‘Pr’ flow and low ‘Pr’ flow.
  1. Blasius Solution / Similarity Eqn or Momentum Equation
    1. Non-dimentionalize the governing eqns and boundary conditions
    1. Decide the similarity variable
    1. Get the final simplified governing equation and Solve!!
