Everything in OpenFOAM!!

OF works in such a way that a certain application/solver is pre-compiled and installed in the workbench. We will get clear picture about the solver later on, but for now let us look at the testCase.

Preparing a test case is very important as your entire simulation is quite dependent. In the testCase you have to mention all the constants, boundary conditions, fvSchemes to use and fv options to solve the equations. (fv- finiteVolume)
Usually one can observe mostly observed folders - "0", "constant", "system". All the initial and boundary conditions are to be stated in the "0" folder. The "constant" folder consists of data (usually constants) to import such as gravity 'g', surface tension, density etc. Finally, there comes the "system" folder which is the core of testCase, core because it contains all the data regarding solving schemes and methods to solve equations.
