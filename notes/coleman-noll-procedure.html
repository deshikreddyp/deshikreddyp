Coleman Noll procedure

The Coleman-Noll procedure is a systematic method for deriving constitutive relations that are guaranteed to be consistent with the Second Law of Thermodynamics (Clausius-Duhem inequality).

Core Axioms

The procedure relies on the assumption that the constitutive Equations must satisfy the entropy inequality for all possible thermodynamic processes.
